After a tough workout,your body needs the right nutrients.The right smoothie can help you recover for the rest of the day.What type of smoothie are you craving?

Smoothies are a tasty way to get nutrients after a workout.The Healthy Green Drink mixes spinach, apple, and cucumber for a fresh and healthy boost.
